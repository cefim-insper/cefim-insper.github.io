The FinanceHub is a community of investment professionals, academics and students in Brazil capable of fostering the research and technology that will help the Brazilian asset management industry cope with its new challenges. Academics from Insper and investment professionals from BWGI were the initial members of this community, but the group now includes participants from many asset managers, banks, pension funds and consulting companies.

Coordinator: Professor Ruy Ribeiro (PhD Chicago)

Members: Guilherme Piantino, Gustavo Torigoe, Josué Costa, Matheus Patrocínio, Mohammed Kaebi, Pietro Consonni and Tomás Nóbrega

This page serves as a centralized hub for storing and accessing research projects in the fields of finance and macroeconomics developed by our professors and students. We invite interested researchers, policymakers, and stakeholders to explore our repository and engage with our work. Whether you are seeking information, conducting research, or looking to collaborate, we welcome your participation and feedback.

If you have any comments or concerns e-mail us at cefim@insper.edu.br
